how to buy

Set Up Your Wallet

Download phantom wallet or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to https://phantom.app/

Get Some SOL

Have SOL in your wallet to switch to GROK. If you don’t have any SOL, you can buy directly on Phantom, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet

Go to Raydium

Connect to Raydium.io Go to app.raydium.org in google chrome or on the browser inside your Phantom wallet. Connect and Paste the GROK mint address into raydium, select GROK, and confirm. When Phantom prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.
